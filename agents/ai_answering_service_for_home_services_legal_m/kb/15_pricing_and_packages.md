# Pricing

## Overview

This document provides an overview of the pricing structure for ZyraTalk, an AI answering service tailored for home services, legal, and medical industries. ZyraTalk offers advanced AI capabilities to automate customer interactions, helping service-based businesses manage appointment bookings, customer support, and lead capture efficiently.

## Pricing Structure

### General Pricing

The specific pricing details for ZyraTalk are not directly disclosed on the website. Prospective customers are advised to engage in a discovery call with ZyraTalk's sales team to get a comprehensive understanding of the pricing tailored to their specific needs and integrations. The pricing may vary depending on the level of customization, the number of integrations required, and the scale of operations.

**Note:** For exact pricing, including any discounts or specific packages, please contact ZyraTalk directly through their website or sales representatives for a detailed quote. [Source](https://www.zyratalk.com/faq).

### Free Trial and Demo

ZyraTalk offers a free trial or demo of their services, allowing potential customers to test the platform's capabilities before making a financial commitment. This trial enables businesses to experience the AI's functionalities in real-time, ensuring it aligns with their operational requirements and customer service goals. [Source](https://www.zyratalk.com/faq).

### Fees

- **One-Time Fees:** These are typically implementation or setup fees due upon the effective date of the order form. They are non-refundable and must be settled before the services commence.
 
- **Recurring Fees:** Subscription-based charges are billed monthly in advance. These fees are not prorated and are charged in full for each calendar month.

- **Usage-Based Fees:** Any additional charges based on the usage of the services, as specified in the order form, are billed monthly in arrears.

### Billing and Payment Terms

Customers are required to authorize ZyraTalk to charge the applicable fees to a provided credit card or through ACH (Automated Clearing House) transactions. It's important to maintain current and accurate payment information to avoid any service disruptions. [Source](https://www.zyratalk.com/terms).

### Taxes

All fees are exclusive of any local, state, federal, or foreign taxes. Customers are responsible for any applicable taxes that may arise from the purchase and use of ZyraTalk's services. [Source](https://www.zyratalk.com/terms).

For precise pricing information and to explore the best options suited for your business, it is recommended to engage directly with ZyraTalk's sales team through a discovery call.
