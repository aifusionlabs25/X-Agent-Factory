## General Questions

**What is ZyraTalk?** 
ZyraTalk is an AI voice assistant platform designed to assist service-based businesses by answering phone calls, booking appointments, and capturing leads 24/7. It ensures that customers receive a fast response even when your team is busy or after hours [Source](https://www.zyratalk.com/faq).

**How does ZyraTalk work?** 
The AI voice assistant and webchat are designed to understand customer intent and respond naturally in real time. ZyraTalk can book jobs, answer common questions, and route calls to your team as needed, mimicking a live receptionist's role [Source](https://www.zyratalk.com/).

**What industries can benefit from ZyraTalk?** 
ZyraTalk is ideal for home service providers such as plumbers, HVAC technicians, electricians, and cleaning companies. It also supports multi-location franchises and businesses in sectors like legal and healthcare, aiming to scale customer service through automation [Source](https://www.zyratalk.com/faq).

## Features & Functionality

ZyraTalk can manage a variety of calls, including appointment bookings, answering frequently asked questions, and providing customer support. It can also handle rescheduling and notifying the appropriate personnel during emergencies [Source](https://www.zyratalk.com/).

**Can ZyraTalk integrate with my existing software?** 
Yes, ZyraTalk integrates with your CRM to sync directly with your booking calendar, dispatch board, or other operational tools, ensuring seamless service delivery [Source](https://www.zyratalk.com/faq).
