8 things you should consider when choosing a veterinarian Are the clinic's office hours compatible with your schedule? How do the veterinarians and staff treat you and your pet? Are the clinic's payment options/plans acceptable to you? If your pet is insured, does the clinic accept your insurance plan? How are after-hours emergencies handled? How do they handle referrals to specialists, if that's necessary? If you have a non-traditional pet, does the veterinarian have experience with that species of pet? A referral from a trusted friend or family member is helpful. Watch Video: Choosing a veterinarian
