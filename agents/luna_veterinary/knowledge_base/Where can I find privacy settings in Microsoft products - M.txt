# Where can I find privacy settings in Microsoft products? - Microsoft Support
Source: https://support.microsoft.com/topic/where-can-i-find-privacy-settings-in-microsoft-products-206f6928-2694-4adf-a50f-9edb95897d81
----------------------------------------

Applies To
Privacy
Here's how to find your privacy settings and other related information in our products.
Note:
To manage settings for some of these products you might need to sign in to your Microsoft account. If you are having trouble signing in, you can
get help with your Microsoft account
.
Popular products
Windows
To manage privacy settings on your Windows 11 device, go to
Start
>
Settings
>
Privacy & security
, or on your Windows 10 device, go to
Start
>
Settings
>
Privacy
.
Microsoft Edge
Select
Settings and more
>
Settings
>
Privacy, search, and services
. To learn more about other ways to protect your privacy in Microsoft Edge, visit the
Privacy in Microsoft Edge support page
.
Xbox
To find out how to change your Xbox privacy settings, see
Manage Xbox online safety and privacy settings
.
Microsoft 365
Manage your privacy settings in any Microsoft 365 app by going to
File
>
Options
>
Trust Center
>
Trust Center Settings
>
Privacy Options
. To learn more, check out our
step-by-step guide
.
Microsoft Teams
For Teams privacy settings on both your browser and app, go to
Settings and more
>
Settings
>
Privacy
. You can also
export your personal Teams data
.
Skype
Select your profile picture and then select
Settings
>
Privacy
.
Find answers to your Skype privacy
questions. You can also
export and delete your personal Skype data
.
LinkedIn
Select your profile icon (
Me
) at the top of your LinkedIn homepage in your browser and then select
Settings & Privacy
. In the LinkedIn app, select your profile icon (
Me
), and then select
Settings
>
Data privacy
. To learn more, see
Managing your account and privacy settings
and visit the
LinkedIn Privacy Hub
.
Bing
Sign in to Bing.com to turn off Search Suggestions and adjust other
Bing privacy settings.
You can also
submit a concern to Bing
.
OneDrive
To learn about how to control who can view your files on OneDrive, read these
best practices for keeping your files secure
.
Outlook.com
To manage your privacy on Outlook.com, go to
Settings
>
General
>
Privacy and data
. You can also
import and export your Outlook email, contacts, and calendar
.
Other products
MileIQ
To learn about how MileIQ works, the kinds of mileage tracking data that's collected, where it's stored, and how to delete it, see the
MileIQ Privacy Statement
.
SwiftKey
For info about the kinds of data that SwiftKey collects, removing your data and account, unsubscribing from product emails, and more, see
Privacy questions and your data
.
Microsoft Rewards
To see your account info and to learn how to opt out of Microsoft Rewards and redeem your credits, visit the
Microsoft Rewards Settings page
.
More privacy resources
Privacy dashboard
To manage your privacy settings and activity data for your Microsoft account,
go to the privacy dashboard
.
Microsoft News Community
To adjust your privacy settings, sign in to Microsoft News Community and select
Privacy
.
Manage community privacy
Apps and services
You can choose the
apps and services that can access your data
.
Ad preferences
Choose whether you would like to see advertising that reflects your interests on the
Ad settings page
.
Marketing preferences
We can
email you a link
to manage promotional communications associated with your account.
